FAQ

Do you take offers on any of your items?

Yes. Offers will be considered on some vintage or discontinued items. Please send your offer to batcitycollectibles@gmail.com for consideration.

What forms of payment do you take?

We currently take Discover, American Express, Visa and Mastercard

How long will it take for my order to ship?

It usually takes 2-3 days to process your order. All orders under 13 oz. will be shipped using First Class Mail via USPS and usually arrive within 1-3 business days. All orders weighing over 13 oz. will be shipped USPS Parcel select and usually arrive withing 2-8 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

At this time we do not ship outside of the United States.

Do you accept returns or exchanges?

All sales are final and there are no returns or exchanges. If you have any questions about an item please ask them in advance of purchasing. You can direct all questions to BatCityCollectibles@gmail.com

Questions, concerns or comments?
